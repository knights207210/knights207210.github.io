Alexa Skills Evaluation

Sikuli Lab, University of Colorado Boulder

In this study, we developed a voice crawler using python and speech-text converter packages to imtate actual human's interaction with Alexa devices and collect responses from 100 most popular Alexa skills under 10 different categories. These responses were evaluated to find out how they comply with current design guidelines published by Amazon. Agendas for future HCI research to improve voice skills' user experiences was proposed.     (arxiv and Workshop IUI'19) [pdf1] | [pdf2]


Deep Learning for VUI

University of Colorado Boulder

In this project, I deploy deep learning methods (e.g. DAN, text CNN, skip-gram) to conduct topic analysis based on my self-collected Alexa skill dataset. This project uses a machine learning algorithm (LR) to generate baseline. Repeat Buyer Prediction Based on User Online Shopping Logs

University of Colorado Boulder

In this project, we developed a framework to predict whether a customer promoted by a big promotion(like "Double 11") will become a repeat buyer or not based on the dataset provided by Tmall.com. In terms of feature engineering, we extracted 129 features(user demographic features, user behavioral features, merchant-related features, user-merchant interaction features, big promotion features, slope features, similarity features, PCA features) and conducted feature ranking analysis. Our final AUC result rannked top 25 in the competition panel.     [project report] | [code]

Contact-free Camera Measurements of Heart Rate under Practical Scenario

Institute for Information Processing, Leibniz University Hannover

In this study, I first implemented the current algorithm framework and improve the current algorithm framework by deploying DLIB method to detect region of interest(ROI) and two-way facial tracking to make sure the effectiveness of ROI. Secondly, new datasets are collected under designed practical scenarios and I analyzed our self-recorded materials qualitatively to conclude a guideline for further studies. Lastly, a ROI-selecting method specifically for head rotation situation was proposed under the direction of the guideline.     [Undergraduate Thesis Report] | [code]
