Personas for Google Cardboard

Posted by Xu on Sep 21, 2019

Google Cardboard is a virtual reality (VR) platform developed by Google for use with a head mount for a smartphone. Users can either build their own viewer from simple, low-cost components using specifications published by Google, or purchase a pre-manufactured one. In this blog, I am going to present three personas around google cardboard headset discussed during the lecture.

Google Cardboard Headset

Google Cardboard Headset Personas

Nick:

Nick

" Hi I'm Nick, I am a sophomore studying in CU Boulder. I am a scooter lover. However, I accidently broke my leg the other day, which will keep me away from classroom for a month. I feel so frustrated about this. I am still eager to keep up with my lectures. I this case, I really hope for some VR devices which could help me get immersive classroom experiences, just like Google Cardboard Headset."

Jane:

Jane

" I cannot express myself very well in front of strangers and I really need to practice my oral skills. " Jane, an 18-year old girl just says that to herself. Jane is new to the college and she is pretty shy. When surrounded with strangers, she is always too shy to express herself. Under this situation, she always wants a scenario where she can practice her oral skills whithout troubling other people. "I think google cardboard headset is a prefect device for me. " said Jane.

Jack:

Jack

Jack is a high school boy and he is a total tech-maniac, interested in all kinds of state-of-art techniques, especially VR. However, he doesn't have too much spare money to afford advanced VR devices. In this case, google cardboard headset is a really fit. He can totally have some first-hand VR experiences via the device without too spending too much.
