Cultural Probe for "DogChat"

Posted by Xu on Sep 28, 2019

Cultural probes helps designers better understand users' experiences which cannot be directly addressed through questions. In this bolg, I recorded our design of cultural probes for "GodChat". Our project "DogChat" is an app-based project, aiming to help dog owners better understand their dogs' needs.

Postcards

We designed three postcards for stakeholders of DogChat. And following questions are listed on each of them:
1. Please list 5 signs that you think can indicate a dog is happy/sad.
2. What would you do if you are away from your dog for a long time?
3. Please tell us about one of your frasutrating experiences interacting with your dog.

Video recordings

For the task of video recording, we will ask dog owners to use a camera to record their daily interaction with the dogs. From these recordings, we will have a better observation about human-dog interaction.
