In-class Activity: Good and Bad Gestures

Posted by Xu on Nov 4, 2019

In this class, we discussed about some good and bad gestures design we meet in daily lives.

Good Gesture Design

  • pinching to zoom (to see someone always)
  • tapping the screen to interact with the button
  • wcrubbing video (up/down, control brightness/volumes)
  • two finger swipe back
  • swipe to delete

    • Bad Gesture Design

  • double top on instagram (to zooming or like), disagreements, overloading
  • Four finger swipe(feedback size differences)
  • can't remap
  • wake Fitbit (unintentional activations, rude af, unambiguous what people are doing)
