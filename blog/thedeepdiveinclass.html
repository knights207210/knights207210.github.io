In-class Activity: Comments on "The Deep Dive"

Posted by Xu on Sep 4, 2019

In class, we discussed about last week's assignment -- reflections about The Deep Dive video. Here are some notes I took:

  • Although the video has been there for a very long time, the design ideas are still effective.
  • We strongly agreed that the silicon valley culture presented in the video could greatly encourage designers open their minds and came up with brilliant ideas. The diversity is the key to design companies.
  • Brainstorming can not only help come up with as many ideas as we can, but also help build structures.
