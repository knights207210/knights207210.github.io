Let's redesign the Apple Remote !

Design charrette discussion result

Posted by Xu on August 31, 2019

In this design charrette, we identified one of the design issues of Apple's Siri Remote: it is designed to be very flat and symmetrical, which is easy to be picked up upside down. We first generated several ideas to redesign on our own, then discussed our ideas and combining them. At last, we documented our solution. Our group members are Tianshu Pang, Youlin Zhou, Pengyan, Yuchen Zhang, Xiaotong Diao and me.

Apple Siri Remote: People put rubber band to tell the orientation.

In our discussion, the main problem to be solved is to make the new remote's upper and bottom part easy to be distinguished. Following this orientation, I am going to summarize our discussion results in three points:

Touchpad to joysticks

Since the bottom part of the remote feels really like the uppper touchpad, we decided to change the upper touchpad into a joystick (like one from xbox controller), which could not only fully function as the original touchpad, but also let users easiy tell between head and tail. At the same time, the joystick is clickable as well.

use different design elements

In order to distingush the two parts, we can apply different design elements, like textures, colors, shapes or thickness. Here are some sketches of the original ideas.

Sketch of the original ideas

After some discussion, our idea evolved into a more mature one by combining these design elements together. We changed the shape of the upper touchpad, the texture and the color of the bottom part. We also added an apple logo at the bottom to help users identify the orientation. The final version looks like the following:

Sketch of the final version

Make the whole remote a touchpad

This idea kind of comes from the design of an iPhone. Originally, we were talking about moving some of the buttons onto the side of the remote. And after discussing about the design of an iPhone, we believe it is also great if we can make the remote a whole touchpad with a home button at the bottom. The home button can activiate Siri as well as start a menu and apple TV's homepage, just like an iPhone's home button. At the same time, the remote's touchpad screen will be lightened when picked up to remind users the orientation. The design details and sketches are as follows:
