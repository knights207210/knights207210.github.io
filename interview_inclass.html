In-class Interview

Posted by Xu on Sep 28, 2019

In class, I interviewed my classmate -- Tianshu, about his hobby. In this bolg, I recorded the questions I designed and the interview results.

First of all, I asked about what did Tianshu do most in his spare time. Under this question, he mentioned that playing video games was one of his favorite. Based on this response, I asked some follow up qestions: 1) What type of video games do you like most (e.g. adventures, strategy, FPS, etc)? 2) Why do you like this type of games most? 3) Which platform do you usually play (e.g. Xbox, console, switch, etc)? 4) How do you get updated information about games (e.g. news, social network, online, etc)?

From Tianshu's answers, we can know that his favorite type of games is strategy games, because he believes strategy games can help strength his mind. At the same time, he mostly uses his Xbox to play these games and he follows some youtube streamers to get updated game news.
